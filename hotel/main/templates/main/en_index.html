MatSaf


Hotel "MatSaf" is located in the Central District of St. Petersburg on the embankment of the Neva. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are both historically and architecturally significant objects of the city, such as the Alexander Nevsky Lavra with a museum of city sculpture and a necropolis, Smolny Cathedral with the Smolny monastery complex, and objects.
